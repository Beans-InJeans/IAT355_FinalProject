The Species at Risk

Nesting Specialist Requires large old-growth branches with thick moss platforms suitable for nesting.
Marine Forager Feeds in nearshore waters, making it sensitive to ocean shifts and food-web changes.

Population Impact

Using the midpoint of the estimated ranges, this visualization shows a decline from about 71,500 birds in 2002 to about 56,500 birds in 2020, which is roughly 15,000 fewer birds.
